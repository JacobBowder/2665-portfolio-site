Jacob Bowder

I'm a young and motivated graphic designer in Lincoln, NE, in which I'm very excited to get my career started. I graduated from the Graphic Design and Media Arts program with an A.A.S. from Southeast Community College in Lincoln. I'm a proud father of a new baby boy and can't wait for the fun that comes with having a new family.

Design Philosophy

My design philosophy can be best described by an analogy my teacher made for me. Would you rather be the designer behind a giant all you can eat buffet or would you rather design the $300 dollar plate that is so meticulously designed with only a few portions on it? I feel as though my best design is that which focuses on the small details. That is what I try to emulate in most of my work, while also trying to break the stride and break the rules every once in a while.
