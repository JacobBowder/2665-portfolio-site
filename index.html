Design Philosophy

My design philosophy can be best described by an analogy my teacher made for me. Would you rather be the designer behind a giant all you can eat buffet? Or would you rather design the $300 dollar plate that is so meticulously designed with only a few portions on it? I feel as though my best design is the design that focuses on the small details. That is what I try to emulate in most of my design, while also trying to break the stride and break the rules every once in a while.
